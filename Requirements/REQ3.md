# REQ3: The Dark Side of the Moon
To cut costs on Moon 99-Deprecated, Garbage Collection Inc. introduced genetically engineered biological solvents to automatically break down the massive piles of tech-waste. It was a spectacular failure, as the solvents mutated into sentient, gluttonous slimes that may consume things in their path. Worse still, a strange radiation of the moon began reanimating the corpses of former workers whose contracts were unceremoniously terminated by being unalive.

## In the moon’s facility, there are several holes (`o`) that can spawn the moon’s creatures every 20 game turns. The following creatures have an equal chance of being spawned from the holes:

- Undead (`Ѫ`): This is a hostile entity that can wander around the moon’s facility with a hit point of 15. If there is a worker within its surroundings, it will attack them with its bare fist, dealing 1 damage point with a 10% chance to hit, though it will completely ignore other creatures. 

- Slime (`⍾`): A non-hostile, gluttonous slime with a hit point total of 25. The Slime cannot attack players or other creatures. Instead, it wanders around the map and consumes some items it finds on the ground. Note that the slime can only consume items that can also be consumed by the workers, including rotten apples, expired cookies, and so on. If an item has an effect after being consumed by the worker, the same effect will also apply to the slime. For example, if the Slime eats a Rotten Apple, it will suffer the same poison effect a worker would, taking damage over time.

#### EDIT (07/04): Slime can consume things directly from the ground. There's no need to put the item into the slime's inventory beforehand.
